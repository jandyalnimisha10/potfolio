Accelerating Gaming Platform Onboarding

The Challenge

When I joined as Product Owner for Integrations, partner onboarding was taking 10-12 weeks from contract signing to going live. This created:

  • There were delays per integration
  • Frustrated partners threatening to choose competitors
  • Constant firefighting and manual intervention
  • Engineering team overwhelmed with support tickets

My Approach

1. Data Analysis First: I built Power BI dashboards tracking every integration phase, identifying bottlenecks in documentation and technical setup.

2. Stakeholder Workshops: Ran sessions with commercial, technical, and operations teams to map the ideal onboarding journey.

3. Self-Service Framework: Created standardized documentation, sandbox environments, and automated validation tools.

4. Iterative Delivery: Shipped improvements in 2-week scrumban, measuring impact after each release.

The Results

  • 67% reduction in onboarding time (10 weeks ‚Üí 8 weeks)
  • 20% reduction in support tickets during onboarding
  • Scaled from 10 to 50+ active integrations

Key Learnings

The biggest win wasn't the technical improvements - it was aligning stakeholders on why speed mattered.

Data-Driven Product Prioritization

The Challenge

Multiple stakeholders (Commercial, Operations, Engineering) were all pushing competing priorities. Roadmap planning meetings turned into hour-long debates with no clear decisions.

My Solution

Created a Prioritization Framework:

  • Built Power BI dashboard showing: customer requests, revenue impact, implementation effort
  • Implemented RICE scoring (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort)
  • Weekly data review sessions replacing opinion-based debates
  • Transparent scoring visible to all stakeholders

The Impact

  • Roadmap alignment meetings reduced from 2 hours to 30 minutes
  • Team velocity increased 20% (less rework, clearer priorities)

Building Compliance-Ready Integration Framework

The Challenge

Gaming operators required MGA, UKGC, and other regulatory compliance certifications before integration. Each jurisdiction had different requirements, causing 3-4 week delays per integration.

My Approach

1. Compliance Mapping: Documented all regulatory requirements across Brazil, Greece, Swiss jurisdictions

2. Reusable Framework: Built standardized compliance checklist into integration process

3. Automated Validation: Created automated tests for common compliance requirements

4. Living Documentation: Maintained up-to-date compliance guides in Confluence

The Results

  • 40% faster compliance reviews
  • Zero compliance failures in production
  • Framework now used across XX jurisdictions
  • Reduced legal review time by XX hours per integration
